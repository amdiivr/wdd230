Overview

Site Purpose:

The purpose of the Bountiful Foods website is to be able to reach more people selling healthy products, not only knowing the product we offer but also knowing the importance of being able to consume these products, also most of these fruits are directly from farmers and through the web you can create your own healthy drink by choosing from the fruits shown on the website, it should be noted that everything will lead us to have a healthier and more fun experience on the website.

Target Audience

The direct audience in the majority according to the survey that was carried out, the majority are young adults, adults and some elderly people with ease of use through the internet, it should be noted that these adults are people with allergies and others with strict diets due to health issues. health, health in general and living in constant activity such as athletes and regular people who do sports can choose to interact with the page, also those who are interested are also welcome to know a little more about this product that is offered in Bountiful Foods.
