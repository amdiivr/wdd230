Tourist Place

This is the city's cathedral, where religious events are held among the catholic people, its construction is as old as the city.

Jirón de la Unión is a street where several commercial stores join and it is very wide so that several people can visit it.

The government palace is where the president of Peru spends most of his time there, also the construction has been intact for several years as the old city.

The classic motorcycle taxi whereabouts is the usual when transporting you through the city, it is not the only transport but it is the best known and fastest.

The green coast beach is very close to the city, we also see how it surrounds the emblematic cities within the Augustinian Chamber of Commerce.
