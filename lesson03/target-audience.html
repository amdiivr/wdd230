Target Audience

Within which we can consider as a target audience would be the entrepreneurs or merchants who would like to place venture or make their businesses known through the chamber of commerce and on the other hand we have the other public that would be the users who enter the page to know about these businesses and also find out about current or future events that occur in the community.

Personas

Nerida Nicasea, Flower and plant sales business.

Nerida, the owner of the Nelays flower shop where she can offer her products both decorative and plant nurseries, during the pandemic sales were difficult but she really likes social networks and within the community she accesses the Augustinian chamber of commerce to see promotions and events in the community.

Ramiro Yupanqui, Urban transport business.

Provides both private and public transportation services, he also supports the community by expediting the transfer to their destination for people who require it, he has been in this business for more than 25 years, he loves to review the community chamber of commerce to find out of new opening locations or businesses that he can recommend to his passengers.

Scenarios

Recommendation of a new commercial premises focused on hairdressing.

Future events to be held within the community to help promote good food care, as well as gymkhanas for the day of the district.

Benefits for being part and active user to the community page.

Support with a network of contacts for recent entrepreneurs.

An academic information activity will be carried out for young people and specialist psychologists will help guide the necessary support for those who suffer from bullying within the community.
